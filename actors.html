Film Director

Christoper Nolan

Best known for his cerebral, often nonlinear, storytelling, acclaimed writer-director Christopher Nolan was born on July 30, 1970, in London, England. Over the course of 15 years of filmmaking, he has gone from low-budget independent films to working on some of the biggest blockbusters ever made. At 7 years old, Nolan began making short movies with his father's Super-8 camera.

Although Christopher Nolan was not the first director of the movie, he was suggested to step into the director's role by his brother, Jonathan Nolan, after Steven Spielberg has left Interstellar without a director.
A few well known movies directed by Christopher Nolan are: Inception (2010), The Batman Trilogy (2005-2012), Man of Steel (2013), Tennet (2020).

Paramount Pictures and Warner Bros. released Interstellar on November 5, 2014, to positive reviews and strong box-office results, grossing over $670 million dollars worldwide.
